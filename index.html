Robotics Information

Robotics is an interdisciplinary branch of engineering and science that includes mechanical engineering, electronic engineering, information engineering, computer science, and others. Robotics deals with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots, as well as computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing. The robotics class at Trinity Christian Academy is taught by Heather Palmer.

This year the robotics club has expanded in great numbers. Heather Palmer is head of the class, and tells the students from doing right from wrong. Also, Heather Palmer got a V5 for the robots for all the kids to see the advancement for the future to come. The V5 gives the students who have expierence with making the robots a lot more fun. Although it may be challenging to others, Mrs. Heather Palmer will help their students. The original robots they had were a grade down, but the new V5 robots are a big upgrade to the robotics class and the students.
