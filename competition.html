Competition

Many competitions take place here at Trintiy Christian Academy. The main competition at the beginning of the semester is a can challenge that occurs when you grab a can and put it in a container to score points. The one with the most cans wins the competition that occured that day. Also, while participating you try to build a robot that can flip caps trhough the first minute. The video that you can click on and see is a competition where they built a robot that could push blocks into a square to score points.

In robotics, each couple weeks you make different styles of robots. When you first start robotics you have to make a robot with a claw extension with it. After building the robot in a certain ammount of weeks, you start doing competitions. As the weeks go by, you disassemble your robot, and add more additional parts to it. When you add the additional parts, Mrs. Palmer gives you more competitions! As the competitions look like they get harder, they are thrilling other kids who enjoy doing it.
